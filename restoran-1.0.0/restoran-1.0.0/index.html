Welcome to Grenadine Caterers, where culinary excellence meets warm hospitality. Delight in a symphony of flavors with our catering expertise!

Our vision

To be a catering hub in Kenya

Our Mission

To inspire healthy living through efficient delivery of quality food and beverage services to our clients.

Our Commitment

We are committed to providing hospitality industry professionals who will make a significant value contribution to our clients by providing catering services beyond expectations.

Our objectives

  • Providing the highest quality meals and services to our clients at reasonable cost
  • Reducing running expenses incurred by the company i.e. payroll expenses and controlled food costs
  • Cater during the company’s special occasions, both in-house and outside catering
  • We believe reasonably priced and good quality meals enhance staff morale playing part in staff motivation.
  • We contribute positively in the building of the company’s corporate image.

Why Choose us.

  • We audit our meat and meat product suppliers to ensure they meet the highest standards, right from the slaughter house. Also, the meat product suppliers have to produce a certifcate from meat inspection officers. They also have to produce a safe layout of the their refrigerated trucks.
  • All our freezers/fridges are monitored daily. That is the use of thermometer to ensure proper and accurate temperature is maintained and proper record kept for reference.
  • The use of proper cleaning material in our entire kitchen. This involves the use of sanitizers in the cleaning of working tables, knives and chopping boards.
  • All our staff goes for medical certificate periodically after six months. This is a requirement for our staff and a proper record is kept to ensure proper renewal.
  • Proper staff uniforms are worn and the trimming of hair and nails etc. for personal grooming.
  • We have trained first aiders and a proper equipped first aid box for any accidental cuts or any other eventuality.
